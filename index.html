About Us

Founded in 2010, Restaurant Delight has been serving authentic Indian cuisine with a modern twist. Our chefs bring decades of experience and passion to every dish.

At Restaurant Delight, we bring you the best Indian cuisine from all parts of India. From the rich, creamy curries of the North to the aromatic spices of the South, we offer a wide range of authentic dishes made with fresh ingredients and love. Come and taste the flavors of India!
